Bife Wagyu

Wagyu beef is often considered the best steak in the world. But cooking it to perfection requires a bit of finesse to maintain its delicate texture and exquisite flavor.

Types of Wagyu

Choosing the right cut of Wagyu can be difficult. Especially if you have a small budget, that would affect entirely the experience. At WagyuBCN shop, offers a range of A5 Japanese Wagyu cuts, each of them with its own unique characteristics.

  • Wagyu Ribeye: Known for its rich marbling and bold flavor, perfect for grilling or pan-searing.
  • Wagyu Tenderloin: The most tender cut, ideal for a quick sear on high heat.
  • Wagyu Striploin: A great balance of tenderness and beefy flavor, perfect for steaks.
  • Wagyu Picanha: Perfect for roasting or grilling, especially in Brazilian-style barbecues.

Preparation

  1. Preheat the Grill: Preheat your grill to high heat, around 450-500°F (232-260°C).
  2. Grill the Steak: Place the steak on the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes per side for a rich sear, depending on the thickness.
  3. Rest and Serve: Let your grilled Wagyu steak rest before slicing to preserve the juices and tenderness.
  4. Serving Your Wagyu Steak: Once your Wagyu steak is cooked, let it rest for a few minutes. Slice against the grain to ensure maximum tenderness. For a truly indulgent experience, serve it alongside roasted vegetables or a light salad to let the steak shine.
