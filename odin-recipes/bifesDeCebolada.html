Bife de Cebolada

Bifes de Cebolada, is a very easy and popular dish between every Portuguese family. Usually it's used pork beef or any juicy beef cured shortly in mostard along with sautéed onions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ingredients

  • 2 big onions, cutted in slices
  • 2 chopped garlic cloves
  • 2 soup spoons of olive
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 cup of white wine
  • 2 soup spoons of tomate sauce
  • salt, pepper of your like

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Preparation

  1. Preparing the stakes: Season the steaks with salt and pepper to taste. Reserve.
  2. Sauté the Onions: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and the chopped garlic. Sauté until the onions are tender and lightly golden, about 10 to 15 minutes
  3. Adding the stakes: Push the onions to the side of the frying pan and add the steaks. Fry them to your liking (bad past, medium or well past)
  4. Incorporate the Ingredients: Add the bay leaf and white wine, leaving to boil to evaporate the alcohol. Then add the tomato pulp and mix well
  5. Cooking: Cover the frying pan and let it cook over low heat for about 15 to 20 minutes, until the meat is tender and the sauce has thickened. If necessary, adjust the salt and pepper
  6. Serve: Serve the hot onion steaks, accompanied by white rice, mashed potatoes or chips, and a fresh salad
